CKD detection services



kidneys are vital organs in the human body responsible for filtering waste products from the blood, regulating blood pressure, balancing electrolytes, and producing hormones that control red blood cell production and maintain bone health.




Kidney health encompasses a range of conditions, from benign cysts to potentially cancerous tumors and the formation of kidney stones. Cysts are fluid-filled sacs, often harmless unless they cause complications. Tumors can be benign or malignant, with renal cell carcinoma being a common cancer type. Stones, solid deposits in the kidneys, cause pain and other symptoms. Understanding these conditions alongside the normal functioning of kidneys, which involves filtering waste and regulating bodily functions, is crucial for maintaining renal health and managing any related issues effectively.
